Reducing impact on the Environment
How can we minimize the impact of computer device production on the environment? What are some ways to reduce the environmental impact of computer use? How can we minimize the impact of computer device disposal on the environment?
Choose a different battery source or different sources of energy. Shut down your PC at the end of the day Not to throw out computers and other types of IT equipment; rather, they should be properly recycled and reused if possible.
