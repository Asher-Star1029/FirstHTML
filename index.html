Harmful impacts on Computer Uses How does computer use harm the environment? What are the negative effects of computer device disposal? What are the environmental consequences of computer device production? -Deplete natural resources. -Heavy Metal Contamination: Computers contain heavy metals including lead, mercury, and cadmium. -Contamination of underlying groundwater or contamination of crops that may be planted near by or in the area in the future.
